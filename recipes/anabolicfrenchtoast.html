Anabolic French Toast

Ingedients

  • Plain White Bread (4 Slices)
  • Egg Whites
  • Stevia
  • Cinnamon
  • Calorie Free Syrup
  • Fruit (Optional)

Recipe

  1. Combine 300 grams of egg whites, cinnamon, stevia into a bowl (to taste).
  2. Dunk 4 pieces of bread into egg white mixture, soaking through.
  3. Cook Bread as French Toast in pan.
  4. Top with Calorie free syrup and fruit to taste!
